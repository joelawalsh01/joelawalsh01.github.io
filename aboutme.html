About Me

I am a current Phd student in STEM Education at UT Austin. Prior to my time in Austin I was a high school mathematics teacher in Los Angeles, CA in Watts and DTLA. My research focuses on the intersection of computing education, community advocacy, and the digital divide. I am currently involved in the creation and implementation of a a high school Quantum Computing course alongside the Quantum Research Center at the Applied Research Laboratories, as well as a number of mathematics and computer science teacher education roles within the Uteach program. Artifacts from these projects can be found within my Github repository. I will be relocating to Los Angeles in 2021, and currently looking for part- time roles in either education or machine learning and data science. CV.
